Overview

Purpose

To increase family clientelle by allowing in families with pets. This should return more revenue because of bigger family parties.

Audience

Target customers:
Scenario 1: Feeling guilty that everyone in the family is going river rafting, but they must leave their dog at home.
Solution: We have rivers at slower speeds so your family dog can join you on the boat.

Scenario 2: Customer has young children who tire easily.
Solution: We have short and slow rapids so the whole family can still get the excitement of riding the rapids, but in a shorter time frame. They can relax along the more beachy areas.

Scenario 3: Customer has a large family so rafting can get pricey.
Solution: With group discounts available, we are able to make it more affordable per person if you come in a large group.
