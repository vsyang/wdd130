We are so excited to have you and your family join us on your next water rafting experience. We have calm rivers for the families who want a tranquil drift downstream, intermediate rivers that gets the blood pumping, advanced rivers for the fearless, and a colossal swim beach to relax and retreat.

For all adventures, we recommend light clothing and/or swimsuits, lots of suntan lotion, and pack some extra towels and change of clothes for the end of the day. We also ask for at least two humans per dog.

Advanced Rafting

This rafting adventure is not for the faint hearted. With 5 miles of white rapids, you never know what's around the next bend! We recommend ages 16+ for this adventure.

*Intermediate Rafting

The rushing waters from our intermediate rivers will get that adrenaline rush going. This river is 5 miles of crazy fun. We recommend ages 10+ for this ride.

*Dogs allowed

*Tranquil Rafting

For our families with young children who want to experience a fun rafting trip. Roughly 3 miles of calm waters that leads directly to the beach at the end of the journey. All ages welcome.

*Dogs allowed

*The Beach

Whether you want to raft first or just relax all day, our private family beach will help rejuvenate you and your whole family. All ages are welcome.

*Dogs allowed
