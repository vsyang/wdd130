White Water Rafting

*Prices:

Adult: $80
Child 17 and under: $60
Dog: $20

*Special discounts applied to groups of 8+

We are so excited to have you and your family join us on your next water rafting experience. We have calm rivers for the families who want a tranquil drift downstream, intermediate rivers that gets the blood pumping, advanced rivers for the fearless, and a colossal swim beach to relax and retreat.

For all adventures, we recommend light clothing and/or swimsuits, lots of suntan lotion, and pack some extra towels and change of clothes for the end of the day. We also ask for two humans per dog.
